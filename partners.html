Conference Partners

Tap to learn more

Global Volcano Risk Alliance

GVRA is an international charity dedicated to mitigating the risk from globally impactful eruptions.

They will lead the theme Exposure, preparedness, and risk reduction. Learn more at the GVRA website.

VolcanoTech

VolcanoTech builds accessible volcano monitoring tools for vulnerable communities. Learn more at volcanotech.co.uk.

They will lead the theme The next generation of volcano monitoring.

ACV

Asian Consortium of Volcanology

ACV unites volcano institutions in Asia for training and hazard mitigation. Learn more at the ACV website.

They will lead the Knowledge-sharing theme and host a panel discussion.

IAVCEI

IAVCEI is the International Association of Volcanology and Chemistry of the Earth’s Interior. Learn more at iavceivolcano.org.

This conference is officially endorsed by IAVCEI.
